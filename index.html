Welcome to Student Gaming
The purpose of Student Gaming is to motivate students to achieve higher academic goals
through sportsmanship, character building, and academic activities, in the ever-growing world of esports.
Benefits of ESports in High School
Being on an esports team in high school brings a number of immediate and long-term benefits. Some are similar to the benefits gained through participating in other high school sports and clubs; others are unique to the expanding world of esports. If you attend an Houston or surrounding area High School, learn how you can join a team at your school
